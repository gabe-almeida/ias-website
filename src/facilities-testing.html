Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you identify asbestos in building materials?

Yes. We identify asbestos in suspect building materials by microscopy with quick turnaround for renovation and due-diligence needs.

Can you test for lead in paint and dust?

Yes — lead testing in paint, dust and water to support renovation, abatement and compliance.

Do you assess indoor air quality and VOCs?

We do. GC-MS-based VOC screening and indoor air quality assessment help resolve occupant complaints.

Can I send a single sample without an account?

Yes. One-off facilities samples are welcome and sampling materials are supplied at no extra charge.
